# 🌊 DARKWAVE Product Design

## 🚀 Overview

DARKWAVE is an AI Assistant & Research Platform designed to deliver intelligent conversations, research assistance, knowledge discovery, and future AI-powered productivity tools.

This document defines the product vision, user experience, interface design, and planned features for the DARKWAVE ecosystem.

---

# 🎯 Product Vision

Create a modern AI platform that combines:

- 💬 Intelligent AI Chat
- 🔍 Advanced Research
- 📚 Knowledge Discovery
- 📄 Document Analysis
- 🌍 Multi-language Support
- 🧠 Personalized AI Experience
