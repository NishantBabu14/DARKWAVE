# 🌊 DARKWAVE Code of Conduct

## 🤝 Our Commitment

We are committed to creating a welcoming, respectful, and inclusive environment for everyone.

## ✅ Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and professional.
- Welcome constructive feedback.
- Help others learn and grow.
- Focus on collaboration and innovation.

## ❌ Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment or discrimination
- Personal attacks
- Offensive language
- Spam or malicious content

## 📩 Reporting

If you experience unacceptable behavior, please open a private issue or contact the project maintainer.

---

🌊 Let's build DARKWAVE with respect, collaboration, and innovation.
